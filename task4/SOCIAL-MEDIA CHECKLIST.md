# Social Media Checklist

## Do’s
1. Maintain a professional tone and language.
2. Verify information before sharing.
3. Respect others’ opinions and privacy.
4. Use strong passwords and privacy settings.
5. Think before posting anything online.

## Don’ts
1. Don’t share personal or sensitive information.
2. Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling.
3. Don’t spread fake news or misinformation.
4. Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content.
5. Don’t click on suspicious links or messages.
