# Stay Safe Checklist

1. Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails/messages.
2. Always verify the sender before sharing personal information.
3. Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts.
4. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
5. Never share OTPs or UPI PIN with anyone.
6. Double check UPI payment requests before approving them.
7. Install antivirus software and keep your system updated.
8. In case of fraud, immediately report it to the national helpline 1930.
